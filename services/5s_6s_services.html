The SSMI® Lean Six Sigma Green Belt

For Industrial/Manufacturing Certification or Services/Commercial Certification are the purest form of Green Belt training available in the world  designed and delivered by the Co-creator of Six Sigma and National Best Selling Author Dr. Mikel J Harry, co-creator of Six Sigma, with decades of successful application of Lean Six Sigma for World's Top Corporates. 

The SSMI® Lean Six Sigma Black Belt

The SSMI® Lean Six Sigma Black Belt Body of Knowledge (BoK) was developed with years of research and contains over 1500+ videos and hundreds of resources for participants to obtain mastery on Lean Six Sigma. The content is constantly updated by a panel of experts to ISO 13053-1, ISO 13053-2 standards on Six Sigma.

SSMI® Lean Six Sigma Black Belt program is the only avenue in the world for an individual to obtain their Lean Six Sigma Black Belt certification directly from Dr. Mikel J Harry SSMI, USA who instituted Six Sigma at Allied Signal,Motorola and General Electric.

Online or In-Person

The program is available on both Online (using MindPro® training system) and Blended delivery methods.

The program also contains additional training on Minitab, JMP and Excel statistical software applications.

The SSMI® Lean Six Sigma Black Belt Program-of-Study is intended to develop technical & business leaders that are capable of propelling their respective organization toward best-in-class status by reducing cost, improving cycle times, eliminating defects & variation and significantly increasing customer satisfaction.

Black Belts are highly trained practitioners who possess the technical knowledge and skills that are necessary to facilitate breakthrough improvements in key processes that support the overall business aims and operational goals of an enterprise. 

Most of today's Lean Six Sigma programs are delivered by the 6th or 7th generation of practitioners, which has lead to inconsistency in program delivery and misinterpretation. In contrast, SSMI Lean Six Sigma program provides participants the correct understanding of the foundations and underlying principals governing the subject.
